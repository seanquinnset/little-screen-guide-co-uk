How we rate

Ratings are based on UK government screen time guidance for under-5s, applied across six factors.

  • Pace is the primary factor. The guidance explicitly recommends slow-paced content for young children. Red pace caps a show at 2 stars.
  • Amber fear factor caps a show at 4 stars — even if everything else scores green.
  • 5 stars is reserved for shows that score well across all six factors with no significant concerns.
  • Episode length is weighted less heavily — parents control the remote, and many shows can be watched in segments.

These ratings are editorial, not official. Disagree with something? Email us — we'd love to hear from you.
